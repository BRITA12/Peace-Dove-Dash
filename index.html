PITCH

In Peaceful Dove Dash you get to be a cute dove on a fun mission to collect peace symbols and spread happiness in a colorful world. Fly around, avoid obstacles, and grab as many peace symbols as you can to put up points. What makes this game really cool is the bright artwork and simple controls so anyone can jump in and play. Each time you collect a symbol you learn a bit about why peace and kindness are super important. So can you collect all the peace in the world? For you to get a heigher score.

THEME

In this game, you control a dove on a mission to collect symbols of peace while avoiding obstacles. As you guide the dove through a colorful and happy world you collect peace symbols that help spread a positive message. But you have to be careful because there are obstacles that can stop you, so you need to dodge them to keep going. The more peace symbols you collect the higher your score will be and you’ll learn the importance of keeping harmony and kindness in the process. It’s a fun and simple way to enjoy a game while sharing a message of peace and its really fun to enjoy it with your friends.
